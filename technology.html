Hardware

Universal Power Quality Controller (UPQC)

⚡

The UPQC is the world's most advanced power quality device, integrating multiple protection and conditioning functions into a single compact unit at transformer level.

Unlike conventional approaches that bolt on costly add-on devices, the UPQC handles voltage regulation, harmonic filtering, reactive power compensation, and fault protection — simultaneously and in real time.

  • Voltage rangeUp to 72 kV
  • Power ratingUp to 50 MVA
  • Response time< 1 ms
  • Efficiency> 99.2%
  • Harmonic filteringUp to 50th order
  • Form factorRetrofit-compatible

How it Works

Precision control in three layers.

01

Sense

The UPQC continuously monitors voltage, current, harmonics, and power factor at microsecond resolution — building a complete real-time picture of grid conditions.

02

Compute

Onboard processing calculates optimal compensation signals using advanced control algorithms, reacting to disturbances before they propagate downstream.

03

Compensate

Solid-state power electronics inject precise correction signals in under 1ms — eliminating voltage sags, swells, harmonics, and reactive power in real time.
