System Design & Specification

🚀

Deployment & Commissioning

Performance Monitoring

How We Work

A structured approach to every engagement.

01 — Discovery

Understand your challenges

We begin with a structured conversation to understand your power infrastructure, business objectives, and key pain points. No assumptions — just rigorous listening and the right questions.

02 — Assessment

Diagnose with data

Our engineers analyse your network data, historical incidents, and energy profiles to build an accurate picture of where losses, risks, and opportunities exist.

03 — Design

Develop a tailored solution

We design a solution specific to your topology and requirements — whether that's a single UPQC deployment, a phased rollout, or a full Aurora network integration.

04 — Deliver

Execute with precision

Our deployment team manages the full project lifecycle, with regular progress reporting and zero-surprise commissioning. We stay until the system is performing to spec.

05 — Optimise

Continuous improvement

Post-deployment, we provide performance monitoring, regular reviews, and ongoing optimisation as your grid evolves — ensuring sustained value over time.
